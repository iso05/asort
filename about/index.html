Milestones

Our Journey

2015

The Idea

Founded in a small warehouse with one product: premium long-grain rice. The vision was simple — bring transparency to bulk food.

2017

First Export

Asort crosses borders for the first time, shipping to three Central Asian markets. Quality speaks without translation.

2019

Full Range Launch

Sugar, buckwheat, and red split lentils join the family. The Asort colour-coded packaging system is born.

2021

ISO Certification

Awarded international food safety certification across all product lines. A promise turned into a paper trail.

2023

40 Countries

Asort reaches distribution partners in over forty countries. From corner stores to national retail chains.

2025

What's Next

Expanding into superfoods and specialty grains. The journey from farm to table keeps getting shorter.
